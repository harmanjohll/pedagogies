Stave Notation

Note Reference

How to Use

Select a note value from the left palette, then click on the staff to place it. Click an existing note to remove it.

Use Play to hear your composition. Notes are played from left to right.

Staff Lines

Treble clef: Lines = E G B D F (bottom to top). Spaces = F A C E.

Bass clef: Lines = G B D F A. Spaces = A C E G.
