Unknown Sample
FB6

Identify the cation and anion by systematic testing.

Procedure Guide
  1. Observe — Note the colour of the solution.
  2. Cation test — Transfer sample to 2 test tubes. Add NaOH(aq) to one and NH₃(aq) to the other, first a few drops then excess.
  3. Anion test — Transfer sample to a clean tube. Add dilute HNO₃(aq), then test with AgNO₃(aq) and/or BaCl₂(aq).
  4. Flame test — Dip a clean nichrome wire into the solution and hold in a Bunsen flame.
  5. Conclude — Identify the cation and anion from your observations.
