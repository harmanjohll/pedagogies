Procedure
  1. Setup — Choose an experiment mode: Reflection, Refraction, or Total Internal Reflection (TIR).
  2. Adjust — Change the angle of incidence using the slider. Observe how the reflected/refracted ray changes.
  3. Record — Click "Record" to log the angle pair. Repeat for several angles.
  4. Analyse — For refraction: plot sin(i) vs sin(r) to verify Snell's law and find the refractive index.
Key Laws

Reflection: angle of incidence = angle of reflection

Snell's Law: n1 sin θi = n2 sin θr

Critical angle: sin θc = n2 / n1 (when n1 > n2)

n = refractive index
θi = angle of incidence
θr = angle of refraction
