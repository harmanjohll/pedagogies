LabSim Practicals / Physics / Pendulum
Procedure
  1. Setup — Adjust the pendulum length using the slider. The length is measured from the pivot to the centre of the bob.
  2. Release — Pull the bob to one side (small angle < 10°) and click "Release" to start the pendulum swinging.
  3. Time 10 oscillations — Click "Start Timer" as the bob passes the rest position. Click "Stop" after 10 complete oscillations.
  4. Record — Click "Record" to log the time and calculate the period T.
  5. Repeat — Change the length and repeat. Collect at least 5 data points.
  6. Graph — Plot T² vs L. The gradient = 4π²/g. Calculate g.
