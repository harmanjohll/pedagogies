LabSim Practicals / Physics / Ohm's Law
Procedure
  1. Setup — Choose a resistor and set the voltage using the slider.
  2. Close switch — Click the switch to complete the circuit. Read the ammeter and voltmeter.
  3. Record — Click "Record Reading" to log V and I.
  4. Vary voltage — Change the voltage and record more readings. Collect at least 5 data pairs.
  5. Graph — Plot V vs I. The gradient gives R.
  6. Calculate — Determine R from the graph gradient. Verify Ohm's law.
