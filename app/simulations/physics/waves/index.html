LabSim Practicals / Physics / Wave Properties
Procedure
  1. Setup — Choose between transverse and longitudinal wave. Set the amplitude and frequency using the sliders.
  2. Observe — Click "Start" to generate a continuous wave. Watch how particles move.
  3. Measure — Use "Freeze" to pause the wave. Identify wavelength, amplitude, crest, trough, compression, and rarefaction.
  4. Investigate — Change frequency and amplitude. How does each affect wavelength and wave speed? Record your findings.
Key Equations

Wave speed: v = f × λ

Period: T = 1 / f

Frequency: f = 1 / T

v = wave speed (m/s)
f = frequency (Hz)
λ = wavelength (m)
T = period (s)
