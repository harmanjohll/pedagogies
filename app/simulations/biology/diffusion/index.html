KMnO₄ Crystal
Potassium permanganate produces a deep purple colour in water. Higher temperatures give particles more kinetic energy, increasing the rate of diffusion.
Agar Cubes

Three phenolphthalein agar cubes (1 cm, 2 cm, 3 cm) will be placed in dilute HCl. The acid diffuses inward, turning the pink agar clear.

SA:V Ratio
Smaller cubes have a higher surface area to volume ratio, so acid reaches the centre faster.
