Procedure
  1. Select metal — Choose a metal block and note its mass (1.0 kg).
  2. Insert heater & thermometer — Place the immersion heater and thermometer into the holes in the block.
  3. Record start temperature — Note the initial temperature before heating.
  4. Switch on power supply — Turn on and note the ammeter (4 A) and voltmeter (12 V) readings.
  5. Record data — Record the temperature every 60 s for 10 minutes (600 s).
  6. Calculate SHC — Use E = VIt and c = E / (m x ΔT) to find specific heat capacity.
